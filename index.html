About Me

Ever since writing my first line of code in high school, I have been hooked. I’ve always been a curious individual who is fascinated with learning how things work and the design decisions behind them. As a child, I remember taking apart my toys and trying to reassemble them, just for the fun of it. Software development has allowed me to foster this excitement for learning and building things for myself as well as others.

Attending Indiana University opened my eyes to the extensive, yet exciting, world of technology. It offered me many new and fascinating resources to fuel my passion. I’ve learned so much through IU, but at the same time, I was exposed to how much I have yet to learn. There’s more problems to solve, more solutions to build, and more lives to impact. And for that, I am very excited for what the future may hold.
