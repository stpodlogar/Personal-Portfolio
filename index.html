About Me

Ever since writing my first line of code in high school, I have been hooked. I’ve always been a curious individual who is fascinated in learning how things work and why they work a certain way. As a child, I remember taking apart my toys and trying to reassemble them, just for the fun of it. Software development has allowed me to foster this excitement for learning and building things for myself, as well as others.

Attending Indiana University opened my eyes to many new and fascinating resources to fuel my passion. Through IU, I’ve learned so much, but I was exposed to how much I have yet to learn. There’s more problems to solve, more solutions to build, and more lives to impact. And for that, I am very excited for what the future may hold.
